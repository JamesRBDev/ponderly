Ponderly

Get to know people better by generating categorically-organized random questions! Ponderly is an application designed to bring people closer together through mutual discussion of various topics

To accomplish this goal, conversation starters and topics are separated into categories that you can select by clicking the associated bubble. Once opened, you will be given a random prompt to ask your friends, family, or love interest!

If you'd like to learn more or keep the discussion going, click the "Generate Another!" button to get a new prompt instantly.
